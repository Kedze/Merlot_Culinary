Chef

Overview:

We are seeking a talented and passionate Chef to join our dynamic culinary team. The ideal candidate should have a strong culinary background, a creative flair, and a commitment to delivering exceptional dining experiences.

Responsibilities

    Menu Planning and Development:
  • Collaborate with the culinary team to create innovative and seasonal menus.
  • Ensure menu items are consistent with the restaurant's concept and target audience.
  • Assist train students at the culinary training center where required.

Kitchen Operations:

  • Oversee daily kitchen operations, including scheduling, staffing, and inventory management.
  • Maintain a clean, organized, and sanitary kitchen environment.
  • Train and develop kitchen staff to ensure consistency and efficiency.
  • Monitor food quality and presentation.

Food Preparation:

  • Prepare and cook a variety of dishes, from appetizers to desserts.
  • Ensure that all food is prepared to the highest standards of quality, taste, and presentation.
  • Adhere to food safety and sanitation regulations.

Staff Management:

  • Train kitchen staff.
  • Ensure staff responsible for stock taking and inventory are up to date on shortages
  • Provide leadership and guidance to the kitchen team.
  • Motivate and inspire the team to achieve excellence.

Customer Service:

  • Interact with customers to ensure their satisfaction.
  • Address any customer concerns or complaints promptly and professionally.

Qualifications:

  • BSc Nutrition, BSc Hospitality Management, or Formal Education in Culinary Arts.
  • Proven experience as a Chef or Sous Chef in a reputable restaurant.
  • Experience in preparing local & continental dishes
  • Strong culinary skills and a passion for food.
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills.
  • Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines.
  • Strong leadership and communication skills.
  • Food safety certification.
  • Ability to work for long hours.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and development.
  • A dynamic and supportive work environment.
  • The chance to work with a talented culinary team.
