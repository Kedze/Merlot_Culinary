About Us

Who We Are

Our story comes with a lot of inspiration from those around us. We chose to make life more simpler and fun for those who get in touch with us. That's why we created Merlot Culinary, a place where food and learning come together in perfect harmony.

Our culinary school is designed to inspire creativity and confidence in the kitchen. With expert instructors and hands-on training, students of all levels can explore the world of food and cooking. From fundamental techniques to advanced culinary arts, our programs are tailored to help you achieve your culinary dreams. And when you need a break from cooking, our food services offer a delicious and inspiring experience. Our chefs craft menus that showcase the best of local ingredients and international flavors, all made with love and care. Every bite is a testament to the power of food to bring us together and make life more enjoyable.

Mission, Vision and Core Values

Our Mission

At Merlot Culinary, our mission is to create unforgettable culinary experiences by combining the finest local and foreign  ingredients with innovative techniques to guarantee continuous client satisfaction and culinary perfection.

Our Vision

To be the culinary brand that symbolizes innovation, excellence, and cultural appreciation in the food industry by becoming a trusted name synonymous with excellent culinary services.
